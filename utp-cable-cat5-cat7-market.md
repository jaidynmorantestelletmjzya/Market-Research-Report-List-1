<p><p>UTP cables such as CAT5, CAT5e, CAT6, CAT6a, and CAT7 are widely used in networking for their ability to reduce crosstalk and external interference. CAT5 cables are basic and affordable, while CAT5e offers improved speeds and reduced crosstalk. CAT6 cables are faster and have better performance over longer distances. CAT6a provides even higher speeds and less interference. CAT7 cables are the most advanced, offering even faster speeds and better performance in high interference environments. Each type caters to different networking needs and environments.</p></p>
