<p><p>Lip fillers refer to the cosmetic products used to enhance or augment the lips. There are two main types of lip fillers available in the market: hyaluronic acid lip fillers and poly lactic acid lip fillers. Hyaluronic acid lip fillers involve injections of gel-like substances containing hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in the body that adds volume and moisture to the lips. On the other hand, poly lactic acid lip fillers consist of injections that stimulate collagen production in the lips over time, resulting in a gradual plumping effect. Both types offer temporary lip enhancement solutions.</p></p>
