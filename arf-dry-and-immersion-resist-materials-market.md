<p><p>The ArF Dry and Immersion Resist Materials market refers to two types of resist materials used in the semiconductor industry for lithography processes. ArF Dry resist materials are used in dry lithography processes, where a gas is used to remove unwanted materials and transfer patterns onto the semiconductor substrate. ArF Immersion resist materials, on the other hand, are used in immersion lithography processes, where the semiconductor substrate is immersed in a liquid. Both types of resist materials are essential for producing highly detailed patterns on semiconductor chips.</p></p>
