<p><p>Environmentally Rubber-Extender Oil Market Types are categorized as TDAE, MES, NAP, RAE, and others. TDAE (Treated Distillate Aromatic Extract) is a popular type known for its low levels of polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons. MES (Medium Extracted Solvate) is valued for its excellent low-temperature properties. NAP (Non-Aromatic Oil Plasticizer) is used as a replacement for conventional rubber-extender oils. RAE (Residual Aromatic Extract) is derived from solvent extraction. Other types include various specialty oils tailored for specific rubber applications.</p></p>
