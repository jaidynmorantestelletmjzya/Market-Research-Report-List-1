<p><p>Industrial tie rod cylinders are used in various applications in industries and are classified into two main types: hydraulic cylinders and pneumatic cylinders. Hydraulic cylinders use pressurized hydraulic fluid to create linear motion, while pneumatic cylinders use compressed air. Hydraulic cylinders are known for their higher force output and precise control, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, pneumatic cylinders are preferred for their faster speed and lower cost, making them ideal for lighter applications.</p></p>
