<p><p>Bio-based propylene glycol (PG) refers to propylene glycol that is derived from renewable biomass sources such as corn, sugar cane, or soybeans. There are two main types of bio-based propylene glycol: Bio-based 1,2- propylene glycol and Bio-based 1,3-propylene glycol. Bio-based 1,2-propylene glycol is traditionally produced through a chemical process, while Bio-based 1,3-propylene glycol is produced through a fermentation process. Both types offer a sustainable alternative to petroleum-based propylene glycol, reducing environmental impact and dependence on fossil fuels.</p></p>
