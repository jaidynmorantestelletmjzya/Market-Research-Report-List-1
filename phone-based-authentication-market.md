<p><strong>The Phone-based Authentication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Factor Certification</li><li>Multifactor Certification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phone-based authentication market includes two main types: Single Factor Certification and Multifactor Certification. Single Factor Certification requires only one method of verification, typically a password or PIN, to access a device or application. In contrast, Multifactor Certification incorporates multiple forms of verification, such as something you know (password), something you have (phone), and something you are (fingerprint or face scan). Multifactor Certification offers a higher level of security compared to Single Factor Certification by requiring multiple layers of authentication.</p></p>
