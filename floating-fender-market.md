<p><ul><li>Solid Rubber Fenders</li><li>Floating Fenders</li><li>Foam Fenders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid Rubber Fenders are traditional fenders made of durable rubber material designed to absorb impact energy during berthing. Floating Fenders are newer technology fenders that rely on internal air or foam chambers to provide buoyancy and shock absorption. Foam Fenders are lightweight and easy to install fenders that use closed-cell foam to provide cushioning and protection. Each type of fender has its own unique advantages and is used in various marine applications based on specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
