<p><p>Handheld scanners come in various types to cater to different connectivity needs. Bluetooth scanners offer the flexibility of wireless connection, making them ideal for use with smartphones and tablets. Keyboard wedge scanners connect directly to a computer via a keyboard port. Serial scanners use a serial port for connection, while USB scanners plug directly into a USB port for quick and easy setup. Wireless scanners offer the convenience of scanning without being tethered to a computer, allowing for greater mobility and flexibility in scanning tasks.</p></p>
