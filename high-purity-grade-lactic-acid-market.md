<p><p>High Purity Grade Lactic Acid market is categorized into three types based on their stereochemistry - L-Lactic Acid, D-Lactic Acid, and DL-Lactic Acid. L-Lactic Acid is the naturally occurring form found in food and beverages and is produced during fermentation. D-Lactic Acid is the mirror image form of L-Lactic Acid and is less common in nature. DL-Lactic Acid is a mixture of both L- and D-Lactic Acid. Each type has different applications in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
