<p><strong>The Free Radical Photoinitiator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cracking Photoinitiator</li><li>Hydrogen Capture Photoinitiator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cracking photoinitiators are types of free radical photoinitiators that undergo a process called cracking when exposed to light, releasing free radicals that initiate polymerization reactions. On the other hand, hydrogen capture photoinitiators capture hydrogen atoms under light exposure, leading to the formation of free radicals that also initiate polymerization. Both types of photoinitiators play a crucial role in various industries where light-induced polymerization is required, such as in the production of coatings, adhesives, and dental materials.</p></p>
