<p><strong>The Reactor Agitators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat</li><li>Bevel Gear</li><li>Helical Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reactor agitators are commonly classified into three types based on the design of their gears: flat, bevel gear, and helical gear. Flat gear agitators have a simple design with straight teeth, making them suitable for less demanding mixing applications. Bevel gear agitators have cone-shaped teeth which provide better torque transmission and are ideal for moderate mixing tasks. Helical gear agitators have spiral teeth that offer smooth and efficient mixing for high-viscosity materials. Each type of reactor agitator caters to specific mixing requirements in various industries.</p></p>
