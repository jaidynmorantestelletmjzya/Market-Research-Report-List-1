<p><strong>The Automotive Camera Module Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Curable Type</li><li>Non-UV Curable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive camera module adhesives market is categorized into UV curable type and non-UV curable type adhesives. UV curable adhesives are cured using ultraviolet light, offering fast curing times and high bond strength. On the other hand, non-UV curable adhesives do not require UV light for curing, providing flexibility in curing methods. Both types offer benefits such as strong adhesion, resistance to vibrations and temperature variations, and durability in automotive applications. The choice between the two types depends on specific application requirements and desired curing characteristics.</p></p>
