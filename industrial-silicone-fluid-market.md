<p><ul><li>Straight Silicone Fluid</li><li>Modified Silicone Fluid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial silicone fluid market can be divided into two main types: Straight Silicone Fluid and Modified Silicone Fluid. </p><p>Straight Silicone Fluid is a pure form of silicone fluid, with no additives or modifications. It is used in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction for its excellent lubricating and insulating properties.</p><p>Modified Silicone Fluid, on the other hand, is a silicone fluid that has been chemically modified to enhance its performance properties. These modifications can include increased heat resistance, improved flow characteristics, or added functionality for specific applications. Modified Silicone Fluids are commonly used in specialty applications where standard silicone fluids may not meet the required specifications.</p></p>
