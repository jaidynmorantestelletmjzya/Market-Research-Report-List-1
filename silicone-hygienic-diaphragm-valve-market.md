<p><p>The silicone hygienic diaphragm valve market can be categorized into two types: weir type and weirless radial diaphragm type. Weir type diaphragm valves are designed with a vertical diaphragm that moves towards a weir or ledge to control the fluid flow. On the other hand, weirless radial diaphragm type valves have a diaphragm that moves radially inward to regulate the flow. Both types are commonly used in hygienic applications, such as in the food and beverage industry, where the prevention of contamination is crucial.</p></p>
