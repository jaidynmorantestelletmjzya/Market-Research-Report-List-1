<p><strong>The Antireflective Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Deposition</li><li>Electronic Beam Evaporation</li><li>Sputtering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antireflective coatings are used to reduce reflections on surfaces and improve optical performance. Three common types of antireflective coating markets are Vacuum Deposition, Electronic Beam Evaporation, and Sputtering. Vacuum Deposition involves placing the substrate in a vacuum chamber and depositing the coating material using various methods. Electronic Beam Evaporation utilizes an electron beam to heat and evaporate the coating material onto the substrate. Sputtering involves bombarding the coating material with high-energy ions to deposit it onto the substrate.</p></p>
