<p><p>Subsea compressors are categorized into two main types based on power capacity: Kilowatt compressors and Megawatt compressors. Kilowatt compressors typically have a lower power capacity, ranging from a few kilowatts to up to 1 MW, and are suitable for smaller-scale subsea compression applications. On the other hand, Megawatt compressors have a higher power capacity, ranging from 1 MW to several MW, and are designed for larger-scale subsea compression projects requiring higher levels of compression power. Both types cater to different market needs and applications within the subsea compressor industry.</p></p>
