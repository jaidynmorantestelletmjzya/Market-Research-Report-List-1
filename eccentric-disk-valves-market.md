<p><p>Eccentric disk valves are available in two main types in the market - wafer type and lug type. Wafer type valves are designed to fit between flanges using bolts and nuts, making them easy to install and remove. Lug type valves have threaded inserts on the valve body to allow for easy installation and removal without disturbing the flange connections. Both types of valves are commonly used in industrial applications where reliable shut-off and control of fluid flow are required.</p></p>
