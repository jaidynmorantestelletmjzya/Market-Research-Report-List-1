<p><p>The Chemicals for IC Substrates market includes various types such as Dry Film, Inks, ENIG (Electroless Nickel Immersion Gold), Photoresist, Etchant, and Contrast Medium. Dry film is used for masking during etching processes, while inks are used for printing circuit patterns. ENIG is a common plating technique for IC substrates. Photoresist is a light-sensitive material used for photolithography processes. Etchants are chemicals used to selectively remove material from substrates. Contrast medium is used for enhancing visibility in imaging processes. Other chemicals in this market cater to specific substrate processing needs.</p></p>
