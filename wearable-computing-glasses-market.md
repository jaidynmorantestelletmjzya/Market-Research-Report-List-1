<p><p>Wearable computing glasses can be classified into two main market types based on sensor integration: built-in sensor and external sensor markets. In the built-in sensor market, the glasses come equipped with sensors like accelerometers, gyroscopes, and heart rate monitors directly embedded into the device. On the other hand, the external sensor market involves glasses that rely on external sensor devices to gather data, such as smart clothing or wearable patches. Both markets offer different advantages and cater to specific user needs in the wearable computing industry.</p></p>
