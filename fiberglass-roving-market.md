<p><strong>The Fiberglass Roving Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-end Roving</li><li>Multi-end Roving</li><li>Chopped Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiberglass Roving Market Types include Single-end Roving, Multi-end Roving, and Chopped Roving. Single-end roving consists of continuous strands of fiberglass wrapped into a single strand, mainly used for applications requiring higher tensile strength. Multi-end roving involves multiple strands of fiberglass twisted or wound together, offering higher efficiency in processing and improved mechanical properties. Chopped roving is made by cutting continuous strands into short lengths, commonly used in compression molding and spray-up applications for improved reinforcement and ease of handling.</p></p>
