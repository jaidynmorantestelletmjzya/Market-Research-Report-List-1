<p><ul><li>Reversible Oxygen Indicator Labels</li><li>Non Reversible Oxygen Indicator Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reversible Oxygen Indicator Labels change color in the presence of oxygen and can be used multiple times, making them cost-effective for monitoring oxygen levels over time. Non-Reversible Oxygen Indicator Labels permanently change color, indicating exposure to oxygen. This type is commonly used for one-time monitoring in packaging to ensure product quality and safety. Both types of labels play a crucial role in the food, pharmaceutical, and electronics industries to prevent spoilage and maintain product integrity.</p></p>
