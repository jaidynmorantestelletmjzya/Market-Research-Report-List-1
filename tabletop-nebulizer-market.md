<p><p>Tabletop nebulizers are devices used to administer medication in the form of a mist that is inhaled into the lungs. There are two main types of tabletop nebulizers - compressor nebulizers and non-compressor nebulizers. Compressor nebulizers use a motor to compress air to create the mist, while non-compressor nebulizers use ultrasonic vibrations or a mesh to convert the liquid medication into a mist. Both types of nebulizers are commonly used in medical settings and by individuals with respiratory conditions such as asthma or COPD.</p></p>
