<p><p>The Maritime Safety System market consists of various types of systems that ensure the safety and security of ships and vessels at sea. These systems include the Ship Security Reporting System, which focuses on reporting any security threats or incidents to authorities. The Automatic Identification System (AIS) helps in vessel identification and collision avoidance. The Global Maritime Distress Safety System (GMDSS) provides emergency communications for distressed vessels. The Long Range Tracking and Identification (LRIT) System aids in tracking and identifying vessels remotely. The Vessel Monitoring and Management System helps in monitoring the performance and safety of ships. Additionally, there are other specialized systems available in the market to cater to specific maritime safety needs.</p></p>
