<p><p>Light duty drawer glides are commonly categorized into four market types: side mount, bottom mount, center mount, and other. Side mount drawer glides are attached to the sides of the drawer box and cabinet, providing stability and smooth movement. Bottom mount drawer glides are installed on the bottom of the drawer box, supporting heavier loads and allowing for full extension. Center mount drawer glides are affixed to the center of the drawer bottom, offering a simplistic design and smooth operation. Other market types include specialty drawer glides designed for unique applications or specific needs.</p></p>
