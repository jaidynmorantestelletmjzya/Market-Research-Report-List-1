<p><p>Water soluble detergent pods are available in two types: PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol) and PVOH (Polyvinyl Alcohol polymer). PVA pods are made from a water-soluble synthetic polymer that dissolves completely in water, making them environmentally friendly and easy to use. PVOH pods are also water-soluble and biodegradable, offering similar benefits as the PVA pods. Both types are popular choices for consumers looking for convenient and sustainable laundry solutions.</p></p>
