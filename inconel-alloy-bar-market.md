<p><p>Inconel Alloy Bar market includes two major types: solution strengthened alloy and age hardening alloy. Solution strengthened alloy refers to a type of Inconel alloy that is strengthened through the solid solution process. This involves dissolving the alloying elements into the base metal to enhance its strength and performance. On the other hand, age hardening alloy is a type of Inconel alloy that gains its strength through a heat treatment process called aging. This process allows the alloy to precipitate fine particles, resulting in increased strength and hardness.</p></p>
