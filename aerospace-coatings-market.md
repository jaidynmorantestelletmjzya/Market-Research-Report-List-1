<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Coating</li><li>Water-Based Coating</li><li>Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace coatings market consists of three main types: solvent-based coatings, water-based coatings, and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings use volatile organic compounds for application, providing a durable finish. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to clean, making them a popular choice for aerospace applications. Powder coatings involve the electrostatic application of dry powder, which is then cured to form a tough coating. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in various aerospace applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
