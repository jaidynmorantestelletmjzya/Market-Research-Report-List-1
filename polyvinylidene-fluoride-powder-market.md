<p><ul><li>Homopolymer</li><li>Copolymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyvinylidene Fluoride Powder market is categorized into two types: Homopolymer and Copolymer. Homopolymer refers to a material made only from polyvinylidene fluoride, while Copolymer is a blend of polyvinylidene fluoride with another monomer, resulting in enhanced properties such as improved flexibility, impact resistance, or chemical resistance. Both types have their unique applications and characteristics, with homopolymers being more rigid and strong, and copolymers offering a broader range of properties for different industrial uses.</p></p>
