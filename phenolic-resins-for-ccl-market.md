<p><p>Phenolic resins for CCL (Copper Clad Laminates) are commonly available in two main forms in the market: powder and flake. Powder phenolic resins are finely ground particles that are easily mixed with other materials for various applications. On the other hand, flake phenolic resins are in the form of small, flat pieces that can be easily incorporated into different materials. Both types of phenolic resins provide excellent thermal and electrical insulation properties, making them essential components in the manufacturing of CCL for electronic devices.</p></p>
