<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) Resin market consists of four main types: Homopolymer, High Crystalline, Block Copolymer, and Random Copolymer. Homopolymer PP is the most common type, offering high stiffness and strength. High Crystalline PP has increased transparency and impact resistance. Block Copolymer PP combines properties of homopolymer and copolymer for improved flexibility. Random Copolymer PP has a balance of toughness and clarity. Each type caters to different applications and industries, providing a versatile range of options for consumers.</p></p>
