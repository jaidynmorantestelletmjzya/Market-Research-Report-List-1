<p><p>Commercial roofing materials can be broadly classified into different types based on their composition and application. Single ply products are lightweight and flexible membranes that offer durability and ease of installation. Modified bitumen materials are asphalt-based products that provide enhanced durability and flexibility. Spray polyurethane foam is a seamless insulation material that offers superior energy efficiency. Built-up roofing consists of multiple layers of asphalt and reinforcing fabrics for enhanced protection. Metals, such as steel and aluminum, are durable and long-lasting options for commercial roofing applications.</p></p>
