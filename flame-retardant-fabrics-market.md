<p><p>Flame retardant fabrics market is segmented into three main types - Halogenated Organic Flame Retardants, Organophosphorus Flame Retardants, and Inorganic Flame Retardants. Halogenated Organic Flame Retardants are compounds containing halogen elements like chlorine and bromine that inhibit combustion. Organophosphorus Flame Retardants work by forming a protective char layer when exposed to heat or flame. Inorganic Flame Retardants are mineral-based materials that act as heat sinks, absorbing energy and delaying the spread of fire in fabrics.</p></p>
