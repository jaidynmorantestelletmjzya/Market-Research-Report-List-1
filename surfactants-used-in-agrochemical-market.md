<p><p>Surfactants are compounds used in the agrochemical market to enhance the effectiveness of pesticides and fertilizers. Nonionic surfactants do not have an electric charge, making them versatile and commonly used. Anionic surfactants have a negative charge and are effective in hard water. Cationic surfactants have a positive charge and are used for soil conditioning. Amphoteric surfactants can have both positive and negative charges, making them useful in various applications. Each type of surfactant offers unique properties and benefits to improve the performance of agrochemical products.</p></p>
