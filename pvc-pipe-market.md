<p><p>The PVC Pipe market can be divided into three main types: Chlorinated PVC Pipe, Unplasticized PVC Pipe, and Plasticized PVC Pipe. Unplasticized PVC Pipe, also known as rigid PVC, is hard and strong, making it suitable for industrial applications. Plasticized PVC Pipe, or flexible PVC, is soft and bendable, making it ideal for plumbing and irrigation systems. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
