Silicon photonics offer advantages such as high integration density, low power consumption, and compatibility with existing CMOS processes. The use of silicon photonics in HPICs enables the mass production of low-cost and highly efficient photonic chips, thereby contributing to the growth of the market.</p><p>Another trend in the HPIC market is the growing adoption of HPICs in telecommunications and data communication applications.
