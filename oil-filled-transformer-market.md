<p><p>An oil filled transformer, also known as a liquid filled transformer, is an electrical transformer that uses oil as an insulating and cooling medium. These transformers are widely used in applications where high voltage is required, such as power generation plants, industrial facilities, and distribution networks.</p><p>The market for oil filled transformers is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
