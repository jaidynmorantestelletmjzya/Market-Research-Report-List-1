<p><strong>The Green Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Produced by Electric Arc Furnace (EAF)</li><li>Produced by Molten Oxide Electrolysis (MOE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Green Steel produced by Electric Arc Furnace (EAF) involves using recycled scrap steel and renewable energy sources to melt and refine steel, reducing carbon emissions. On the other hand, Green Steel produced by Molten Oxide Electrolysis (MOE) utilizes green hydrogen as a reducing agent to extract iron from its ores, further lowering emissions. Both methods offer environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional steel production, meeting the growing demand for sustainable steel solutions in various industries.</p></p>
