<p><p>Zirconium silicate beads are widely used in various industries including ceramics, coatings, and pharmaceuticals. There are two main types of zirconium silicate beads - sintered zirconium silicate and fused zirconium silicate. Sintered zirconium silicate beads are produced by compacting and heating zirconium silicate powder, resulting in a durable and high-density bead. Fused zirconium silicate beads, on the other hand, are created by melting zircon sand and fusing it into spherical beads. Both types have different characteristics and applications in the market.</p></p>
