<p><p>Respiratory therapeutic devices market consists of two main types: nebulizers and humidifiers. Nebulizers are devices used to deliver medication in the form of a mist or aerosol, making it easier for patients to inhale and treat respiratory conditions. On the other hand, humidifiers add moisture to the air to prevent dryness and irritation in the respiratory system. Both nebulizers and humidifiers play a crucial role in managing respiratory conditions and improving overall respiratory health.</p></p>
