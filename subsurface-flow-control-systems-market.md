<p><strong>The Subsurface Flow Control Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outflow Control</li><li>Inflow Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subsurface flow control systems can be categorized into two main types: Outflow control and Inflow control. Outflow control systems regulate the drainage or release of water from the subsurface, helping to manage water levels and prevent flooding. On the other hand, Inflow control systems manage the entry or infiltration of water into the subsurface, helping to protect infrastructure and prevent water damage. Both types play a crucial role in effectively managing water flow and preserving the integrity of subsurface structures.</p></p>
