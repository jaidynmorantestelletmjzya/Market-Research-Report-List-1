<p><p>UV cured adhesives are classified into different types based on their chemical composition. The main types include Silicone, Urethane, Acrylic, Epoxy, and Others. Silicone adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to high temperatures, while Urethane adhesives provide excellent durability and strength. Acrylic adhesives are known for their fast curing time and versatility in various applications. Epoxy adhesives offer high bonding strength and chemical resistance. Other types of UV cured adhesives include hybrid formulations that combine different properties for specific applications.</p></p>
