<p><p>Flexible packaging adhesives technology market is categorized into five major types: water-based, solvent-based, hot-melt, solvent-free, and other. Water-based adhesives are formulated with water as a carrier and are environmentally friendly. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents and provide excellent adhesion, but may have harmful effects on human health and the environment. Hot-melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and require heat to melt and form a bond. Solvent-free adhesives do not contain any solvents, making them safe and eco-friendly. The "other" category includes special formulations or hybrid adhesives with unique properties for specific applications in the flexible packaging industry.</p></p>
