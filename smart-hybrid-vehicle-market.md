<p><p>Smart Hybrid Vehicle Market Types include Belt-integrated Starter Generator and Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator. The Belt-integrated Starter Generator system uses the engine belt to generate electricity and assist the engine during acceleration, while the Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator is directly connected to the engine crankshaft to provide electric power. Both systems help improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions by supplementing the internal combustion engine with electric power, making them popular choices in the market for smart hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
