<p><p>Solder fluxes are categorized into different types based on their properties and applications. Water soluble fluxes are easily removed with water after soldering, making them environmentally friendly. No-clean fluxes do not require cleaning after soldering, reducing production time and costs. Other types of fluxes include rosin-based, organic, and inorganic fluxes, each offering specific benefits for different soldering processes. Manufacturers choose the appropriate solder flux type based on factors such as desired residue level, compatibility with other materials, and environmental regulations.</p></p>
