<p><p>Darkroom lamps are specialized lighting sources used in darkrooms for developing photographs. They come in various types such as red, white, and blue darkroom lamps. Red darkroom lamps are commonly used as they provide the least amount of light that won't affect light-sensitive materials. White darkroom lamps are used for general lighting purposes, while blue darkroom lamps are used for specialized color printing. Other markets for darkroom lamps may include green or yellow lamps, which are used for alternative printing processes or specific color manipulation in photography.</p></p>
