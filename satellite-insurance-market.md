<p><ul><li>Ground risk</li><li>Satellite risk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Satellite insurance market typically includes two types: ground risk and satellite risk. Ground risk refers to potential damages to the satellite while it is on Earth, such as during its transportation or testing. Satellite risk, on the other hand, covers damages or losses that the satellite may experience once it is in space, including collisions, in-orbit failures, or even loss of communication. These two market types provide coverage against different risks and ensure financial protection for satellite operators and owners.</p></p>
