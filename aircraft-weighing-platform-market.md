<p><p>The Aircraft Weighing Platform market is categorized into two types: Digital and Analog. Digital weighing platforms use technology to convert the weight measurements into digital signals that can be easily read and recorded by a digital display. This type of platform offers precision and accuracy in weight readings. On the other hand, Analog weighing platforms use mechanical mechanisms to measure the weight and display it using a traditional analog scale. While analog platforms may lack the advanced features of digital platforms, they can still provide accurate weight measurements.</p></p>
