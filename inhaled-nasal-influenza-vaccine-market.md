<p><p>Inhaled nasal influenza vaccines are available in two main types: Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine and Split Influenza Vaccine. Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine contains a weakened form of the virus that is able to replicate in the nasal passage without causing illness. Split Influenza Vaccine, on the other hand, contains purified viral proteins that stimulate an immune response without causing infection. Both types of vaccines are administered through nasal spray and provide protection against seasonal influenza strains.</p></p>
