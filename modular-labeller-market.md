<p><p>Straight Line Labeling Machines are designed for labeling products in a straight line direction on production lines. They are ideal for applying labels to products that are moving in a linear manner. </p><p>Rotary Labeling Machines are specifically designed to label products that are rotating or moving in a circular motion along the production line. These machines are commonly used for labeling bottles, cans, jars, and other cylindrical containers. </p><p>Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and manufacturing for efficient and accurate labeling of products.</p></p>
