<p><ul><li>Ferroniobium</li><li>Nickel Niobium</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nb alloys are divided into two main market types - Ferroniobium and Nickel Niobium. Ferroniobium is a ferroalloy composed of iron and niobium, primarily used in the production of high-strength low-alloy steels. It improves the strength, toughness, and corrosion resistance of the steel. On the other hand, Nickel Niobium is an alloy consisting of nickel and niobium, commonly used in specialty steels and superalloys. These materials are important in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction due to their unique properties and performance benefits.</p></p>
