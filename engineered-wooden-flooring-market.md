<p><p>Engineered wooden flooring comes in two main types: Three Layers Engineered Wooded Flooring and Multilayer Engineered Wooded Flooring. Three Layers Engineered Wooded Flooring consists of three layers of wood, with the top layer being solid wood and the bottom layers made of plywood or other engineered wood. Multilayer Engineered Wooded Flooring, on the other hand, has multiple layers of wood, with each layer varying in thickness and composition. Both types offer durability, stability, and a wide range of design options for consumers.</p></p>
