<p><p>Soft gaskets are commonly classified into two main types based on the material used in their construction: semi-metallic and non-metallic. Semi-metallic gaskets are made from a combination of metal and non-metallic materials, providing a balance of strength and flexibility. On the other hand, non-metallic gaskets are typically composed of materials such as rubber, cork, or graphite, offering good sealing properties and chemical resistance. Both types cater to different industrial applications based on factors like temperature, pressure, and media compatibility.</p></p>
