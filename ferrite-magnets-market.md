<p><ul><li>Sintered Ferrite Magnet</li><li>Bonded Ferrite Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ferrite magnets are available in two main types - sintered ferrite magnets and bonded ferrite magnets. Sintered ferrite magnets are made through a process of compacting and heating powdered ferrite material, resulting in a strong and durable magnet with high magnetic properties. Bonded ferrite magnets, on the other hand, are made by mixing ferrite powder with a binder and pressing into the desired shape. These magnets are less brittle and can be manufactured into more intricate shapes. Both types of magnets are widely used in various industries for their magnetic properties.</p></p>
