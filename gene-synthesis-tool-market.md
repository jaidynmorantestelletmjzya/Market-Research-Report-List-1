<p><p>The Gene Synthesis Tool market consists of various types such as oligonucleotides, enzymes, cloning technology kits, chassis organisms, and xeno-nucleic acids (XNO). Oligonucleotides are short DNA/RNA sequences used in gene synthesis and molecular diagnostics. Enzymes are proteins that catalyze biochemical reactions, crucial for DNA replication and gene cloning. Cloning technology kits contain essential tools and reagents for gene cloning and expression. Chassis organisms are living cells used as a platform for genetic modification and engineering. Xeno-nucleic acids (XNO) are synthetic DNA/RNA analogs capable of storing and transferring genetic information, enabling novel applications in synthetic biology.</p></p>
