<p><p>Fat liquor is used in leather production to soften the leather and increase its flexibility by lubricating the fibers. There are four main types of fat liquor in the market: synthetic fat liquor, semi-synthetic fat liquor, vegetable-based fat liquor, and fish oil-based fat liquor. Synthetic fat liquor is chemically manufactured, semi-synthetic fat liquor is a blend of synthetic and natural components, vegetable-based fat liquor is derived from plant sources, and fish oil-based fat liquor is extracted from fish oil. Each type offers different properties and benefits for leather processing.</p></p>
