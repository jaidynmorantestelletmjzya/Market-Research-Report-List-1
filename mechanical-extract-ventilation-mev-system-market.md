<p><p>Mechanical Extract Ventilation (MEV) systems are classified into two main types: decentralized and centralized. Decentralized MEV systems have individual units installed in specific rooms or areas, providing localized ventilation control. On the other hand, centralized MEV systems consist of a single unit that serves the entire building, using ductwork to extract air from multiple rooms. Both types offer effective ventilation solutions for residential and commercial buildings, providing improved indoor air quality and energy efficiency.</p></p>
