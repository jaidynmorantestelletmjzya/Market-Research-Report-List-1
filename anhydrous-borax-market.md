<p><p>Anhydrous Borax market includes two main types: Burning Borax and Fusing Hydrated Borax. Burning Borax involves heating hydrated borax to drive off water molecules, resulting in an anhydrous form. This process is commonly used in industrial applications. On the other hand, Fusing Hydrated Borax involves melting hydrated borax to form a glass-like substance, which is used in various industries such as ceramics and glass manufacturing. Both processes play a crucial role in the anhydrous borax market.</p></p>
