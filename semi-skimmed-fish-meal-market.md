<p><p>Semi-skimmed fish meal is categorized into two market types: flame dried (FD) fish meal and steam dried (SD) fish meal. Flame dried fish meal is produced by using direct flame to dry the fish ingredients, while steam dried fish meal is produced through steam cooking before drying. Both processes remove moisture from the fish to create a protein-rich meal. However, the method used in each type results in variations in texture, aroma, and nutrient content of the fish meal, thus potentially appealing to different market preferences.</p></p>
