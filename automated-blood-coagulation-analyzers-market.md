<p><p>Automated blood coagulation analyzers are devices used in laboratories to analyze the process of blood clot formation, known as coagulation. There are two main types of these analyzers: fully automated devices and semi-automated devices. Fully automated devices carry out the entire coagulation test process automatically, from sample processing to result interpretation. This allows for higher accuracy and efficiency. On the other hand, semi-automated devices require some manual steps, such as sample preparation, while other steps are automated. Both types of analyzers contribute to improving the diagnosis and management of blood clotting disorders.</p></p>
