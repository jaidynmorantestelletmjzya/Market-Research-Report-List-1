<p><ul><li>Mapping</li><li>Non-Mapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS bike computers market can be divided into two types: mapping and non-mapping. Mapping bike computers offer the ability to display maps and show routes during cycling. They also provide turn-by-turn directions and allow users to create custom routes. On the other hand, non-mapping bike computers focus on tracking basic data such as speed, distance, time, and heart rate without displaying maps or routes. These devices cater to different needs and preferences among cyclists, offering options for those who value navigation assistance or those who prioritize performance tracking.</p></p>
