<p><p>Scuba fins come in two main market types: open heeled fins and closed heeled (full foot) fins. Open heeled fins are designed with an adjustable strap that wraps around the back of the heel, allowing for a customizable fit and easy donning and doffing. Closed heeled fins, on the other hand, have a full foot pocket that encases the entire foot, providing a more secure and streamlined fit. Each type of fin caters to different preferences and diving conditions, offering divers a variety of options to choose from.</p></p>
