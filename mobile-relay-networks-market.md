<p><p>Mobile relay networks can be categorized into two main types: context-based and dissemination-based markets. In context-based markets, information is relayed based on specific contextual factors such as location or user preferences. On the other hand, dissemination-based markets focus on broadcasting information to a wide audience without considering individual contexts. Both types of markets play a crucial role in improving communication reliability and coverage in mobile networks by efficiently relaying data to the intended recipients.</p></p>
