USP 2-0 to 10-0 sutures are thicker and stronger, suitable for robust tissue closure, while USP 1 and USP 2 sutures are finer and more delicate, ideal for delicate procedures or tissues. Surgeons can choose the appropriate size based on the specific requirements of the surgical procedure.</p></p>
