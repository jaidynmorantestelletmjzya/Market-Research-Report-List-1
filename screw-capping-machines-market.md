<p><p>Screw capping machines are available in three main market types based on their level of automation: Manual, Semi-Automatic, and Automatic machines. Manual capping machines require human operation for every cap placed, making them suitable for smaller-scale production. Semi-Automatic machines partially automate the capping process, reducing manual labor while offering more efficiency than manual cappers. Automatic capping machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention and are ideal for high-volume production lines where speed and precision are essential.</p></p>
