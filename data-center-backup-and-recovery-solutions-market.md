<p><p>In the data center backup and recovery solutions market, there are two types of markets: active and passive. The active market refers to products and services that are actively utilized by organizations to back up and recover their data. This includes technologies such as backup software, hardware, and cloud-based solutions. On the other hand, the passive market involves the storage and archival of data for long-term protection.
