<p><p>Product configurator software is available in two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based product configurator software is hosted and operated on a remote server, allowing users to access it through the internet. This option offers flexibility, scalability, and ease of implementation, making it popular among small and medium-sized businesses. On the other hand, on-premises product configurator software requires installation on local servers and is managed internally by the organization. This choice provides greater control, data security, and customization options, but requires more resources and maintenance.</p></p>
