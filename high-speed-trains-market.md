<p><p>High Speed Trains market consists of two main types: Wheel on Rail and Maglev. Wheel on Rail trains use conventional steel wheels running on tracks, providing a smooth and reliable ride at high speeds. Maglev trains, on the other hand, use magnetic levitation technology, creating a frictionless environment which allows for even higher speeds and smoother rides. Both markets cater to different consumer needs, with Wheel on Rail being more common and established, while Maglev offers cutting-edge technology for futuristic transportation solutions.</p></p>
