<p><p>Natural industrial adhesives can be categorized into three main types. The first type is vegetable starch, specifically dextrin, which is derived from plants and is widely used in packaging and paper industries. The second type is natural resins, which are plant-based substances that are commonly used in woodworking and construction. The third type is animal-based adhesives, which are sourced from animals like bees and are used in industries like food packaging and bookbinding. Each of these types offers unique properties and benefits for various industrial applications.</p></p>
