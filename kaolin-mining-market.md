<p><strong>The Kaolin Mining Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-washed</li><li>Airfloat</li><li>Calcined</li><li>Delaminated</li><li>Surface-modified & Unprocessed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kaolin mining market includes various types of products based on the processing techniques used. Water-washed kaolin is obtained by washing the raw material to remove impurities, resulting in a refined product. Airfloat kaolin is produced by flotation, generating a fine particle size for high brightness and smoothness. Calcined kaolin is obtained by heating the material, resulting in increased brightness and whiteness. Delaminated kaolin is processed to separate the layers, resulting in improved opacity and ink receptivity. Surface-modified kaolin undergoes chemical modifications to enhance properties. Unprocessed kaolin refers to the raw material as it is found in nature.</p></p>
