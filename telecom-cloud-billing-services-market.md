<p><p>Telecom Cloud Billing Services refers to the various types of billing solutions used by telecommunications companies in cloud environments. </p><p>Embedded Billing involves integrating billing functionalities within other telecom applications, providing a unified system. </p><p>Convergent Billing consolidates multiple service charges into a single bill, simplifying the process for customers. </p><p>Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Billing enables telecom providers to directly bill their end-users without involving intermediaries. </p><p>Prepaid Billing allows customers to pay for telecommunications services upfront, controlling their expenses and avoiding unforeseen charges. These different market types offer telecom companies flexible and efficient billing solutions for improved customer experience.</p></p>
