<p><strong>The 3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline AOI</li><li>Offline AOI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems can be categorized into two main types: Inline AOI and Offline AOI. Inline AOI systems are integrated directly into the manufacturing line to inspect products in real time, ensuring immediate feedback and correction of defects. Offline AOI systems, on the other hand, are separate from the manufacturing line and are typically used for more in-depth inspections or as a secondary inspection process. Both types of AOI systems play important roles in ensuring the quality and reliability of manufactured products.</p></p>
