<p><p>Vertical waste baling machines are available in two main types - semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention to load the waste into the machine and remove the bales once they are compacted. Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, are equipped with sensors and controls that automate the entire baling process, from loading to bale ejection. Each type caters to different needs and budgets, with fully automatic machines offering greater efficiency and convenience.</p></p>
