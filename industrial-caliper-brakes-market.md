<p><p>Industrial caliper brakes market types can be categorized by product type, including floating type and fixed type brakes. They can also be classified by driven type, such as hydraulic or pneumatic brakes. Floating caliper brakes are designed to move laterally to apply pressure evenly on both sides of the rotor, while fixed caliper brakes have pistons on both sides of the rotor. Hydraulic brakes use fluid pressure to apply force, while pneumatic brakes use air pressure.</p></p>
