<p><p>Quantum Dot Display (QLED) is a type of display technology that utilizes quantum dots to enhance picture quality. There are two primary market types in the QLED market: QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) and QLED. QDEF is a film containing quantum dots that is applied to an LCD display to improve color and brightness performance. On the other hand, QLED refers to displays that directly integrate quantum dots into the display, resulting in higher color accuracy and better black levels. Both QDEF and QLED aim to provide superior color reproduction and overall picture quality compared to traditional LCD displays.</p></p>
