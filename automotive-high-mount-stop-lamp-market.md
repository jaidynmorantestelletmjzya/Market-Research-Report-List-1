<p><p>Automotive High-Mount Stop Lamps can be categorized into three types: LED High Level Brake Lamp, LED Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp, and Centre High Mounted Stop Lamp. LED High Level Brake Lamps are modern and energy-efficient brake lights that are mounted at the top of the rear windshield. LED Centre High Mounted Stop Lamps are similar but placed in the center of the rear windshield. Centre High Mounted Stop Lamps are traditional brake lights positioned at the top of the rear window. These variations cater to different preferences and requirements of car manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
