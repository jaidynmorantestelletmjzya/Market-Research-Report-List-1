<p><p>Drain cleaners are classified into three main types based on their active ingredients: Caustic drain cleaners, Oxidizing drain cleaners, and Acid drain cleaners. Caustic drain cleaners work by breaking down grease and organic materials, oxidizing drain cleaners use chemicals like bleach to break down blockages, while acid drain cleaners are effective against mineral deposits and other tough clogs. Each type of drain cleaner has its own unique benefits and risks, so it's important to choose the right one for the specific type of blockage you are dealing with.</p></p>
