<p><p>Black masterbatch is categorized into different market types based on the type of polymer it is designed to be used with. PE black masterbatch is specifically formulated for use with polyethylene (PE) resins, while PP black masterbatch is designed for polypropylene (PP) resins. PS black masterbatch is meant for polystyrene (PS) resins. Other types of black masterbatch are formulated for use with various other polymer resins, allowing for customization and versatility in different applications.</p></p>
