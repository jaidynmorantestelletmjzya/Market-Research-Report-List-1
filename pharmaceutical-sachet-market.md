<p><ul><li>Single Dose Sachet</li><li>Double Dose Sachet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical sachets are available in two main types: single dose and double dose sachets. Single dose sachets contain a pre-measured amount of medication for one-time use, making them convenient and easy to carry. Double dose sachets, on the other hand, contain a larger amount of medication meant for two separate doses. Both types of sachets are popular in the pharmaceutical market due to their convenience, accuracy in dosage, and ability to improve medication compliance among patients.</p></p>
