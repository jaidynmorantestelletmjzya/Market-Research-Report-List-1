<p><p>Medical valves are crucial devices used in various medical procedures. One type is the one-way valve, which allows only unidirectional flow of fluids or air, preventing any backflow. This is particularly useful in respiratory equipment or infusion systems. On the other hand, multi-way valves enable the control of multiple fluid pathways, providing physicians with flexibility when performing complex procedures. By regulating the direction and flow of fluids, both types of valves ensure safety and precision in medical treatments.</p></p>
