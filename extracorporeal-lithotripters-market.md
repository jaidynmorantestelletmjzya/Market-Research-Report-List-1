<p><p>Extracorporeal lithotripters are medical devices used for non-invasive treatment of kidney stones. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1) Lithotripsy Table: This type of lithotripter includes a specialized table where the patient lies during the procedure. It aids in positioning the patient to ensure accurate targeting of the kidney stone(s) using shockwaves.</p><p>2) C-arm: These lithotripters are equipped with a C-shaped arm that contains imaging equipment. This allows real-time image guidance to locate and target the stone, resulting in more precise treatment.</p><p>3) Endoscopy Column: This type of lithotripter includes an endoscopy column, which allows an endoscope to be inserted into the patient's body. This enables direct visualization of the stone and its removal, improving treatment outcomes.</p></p>
