<p><p>The fiber ureteroscope market is divided into three types: flexible ureteroscopes, semi-rigid ureteroscopes, and rigid ureteroscopes. Flexible ureteroscopes are highly maneuverable with a deflection range, allowing for enhanced navigation through the urinary tract. Semi-rigid ureteroscopes offer a balance between flexibility and rigidity, suitable for accessing various parts of the ureter. Rigid ureteroscopes are inflexible but provide excellent image quality and durability for performing complex procedures. Each type caters to different requirements, enabling urologists to choose the most suitable instrument based on their specific needs and patient conditions.</p></p>
