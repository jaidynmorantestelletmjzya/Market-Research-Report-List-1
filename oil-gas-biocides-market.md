<p><ul><li>Oxidizing Fungicide</li><li>Non-Oxidizing Fungicide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil & Gas Biocides market can be divided into two main types: Oxidizing Fungicides and Non-oxidizing Fungicides. Oxidizing Fungicides work by releasing reactive oxygen species that can destroy microorganisms, while Non-oxidizing Fungicides work by disrupting the cellular structures of the microorganisms. Both types of biocides are used in the oil and gas industry to prevent microbial growth and contamination in pipelines, tanks, and drilling equipment. The choice between the two types depends on factors such as the specific application and environmental considerations.</p></p>
