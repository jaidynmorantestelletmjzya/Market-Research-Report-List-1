<p><p>Non-thermal processing methods such as High Pressure Processing (HPP), Pulsed Electric Field (PEF), Irradiation, Ultrasound, and Cold Plasma are gaining traction in the food industry due to their ability to extend shelf life and maintain food quality. HPP involves subjecting food products to high pressures, PEF uses electric pulses to disrupt cell membranes, irradiation involves exposing food to ionizing radiation, ultrasound uses sound waves to disrupt cell structures, and cold plasma utilizes ionized gases to inactivate microorganisms. These methods offer innovative alternatives to traditional heat-based processing techniques.</p></p>
