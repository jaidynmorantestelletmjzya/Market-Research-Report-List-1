<p><p>The Cruciate Ligament Repair market consists of two types: Anterior Cruciate Ligament (ACL) Repair and Posterior Cruciate Ligament (PCL) Repair. ACL repair involves the reconstruction or repair of the ligament located in the center of the knee, as it is prone to injury during sports or accidents. PCL repair, on the other hand, focuses on the reconstruction or repair of the ligament at the back of the knee. Both types of surgeries aim to restore stability and functionality to the knee joint following ligament damage.</p></p>
