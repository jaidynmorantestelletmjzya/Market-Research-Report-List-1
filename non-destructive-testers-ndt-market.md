<p><p>Non Destructive Testers (NDT) market includes various types like Magnetic Particle Tester, Ultrasonic Tester, Liquid Penetrant Tester, and Others. Magnetic Particle Tester uses magnetic fields to detect surface and subsurface defects in materials. Ultrasonic Tester uses high-frequency sound waves to inspect materials for internal flaws. Liquid Penetrant Tester involves applying a liquid dye to surfaces to reveal surface flaws. Other types of NDT methods include radiographic testing, eddy current testing, and visual inspection among others. These methods help ensure the quality and integrity of materials without causing damage.</p></p>
