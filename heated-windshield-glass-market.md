<p><p>Heated windshield glass is available in two main types: Heated Wire Windshield and Heated Coated Windshield. Heated Wire Windshield consists of a thin wire running through the glass that heats up to remove ice and fog. On the other hand, Heated Coated Windshield has a special conductive coating on the glass that generates heat when an electric current passes through it. Both types of heated windshield glass are designed to improve visibility and safety by preventing ice and fog buildup.</p></p>
