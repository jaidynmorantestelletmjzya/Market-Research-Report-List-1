<p><ul><li>Restaurant-controlled</li><li>Independent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online food ordering system market can be categorized into two types: restaurant-controlled and independent markets. In a restaurant-controlled market, the online platform is developed and managed by the restaurant itself. Customers place their orders directly through the restaurant's website or app. On the other hand, in an independent market, third-party platforms act as intermediaries connecting customers with multiple restaurants. Customers can choose from a variety of restaurants and place orders through these platforms. Both types cater to the growing demand for convenient and hassle-free food ordering services.</p></p>
