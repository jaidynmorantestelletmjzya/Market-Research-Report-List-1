<p><strong>The Flight Data Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>On Board</li><li>On Ground</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flight Data Monitoring System (FDMS) market is categorized into two types: On Board and On Ground. On Board FDMS involves the installation of equipment on the aircraft to collect and analyze flight data in real-time during flight operations. It helps in identifying potential safety risks and improving operational efficiency. On the other hand, On Ground FDMS refers to the analysis of flight data after the aircraft has landed. It is used for post-flight analysis, safety reporting, and identifying trends or patterns for proactive safety measures. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing aviation safety.</p></p>
