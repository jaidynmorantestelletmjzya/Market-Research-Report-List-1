Raw particleboard is the standard type and is used in a variety of applications. Fire resistant particleboard is treated to be more resistant to fire, making it ideal for use in buildings that require higher levels of fire safety. Moisture resistant particleboard is designed to withstand moisture and is commonly used in areas where moisture exposure is a concern, such as kitchens and bathrooms.</p></p>
