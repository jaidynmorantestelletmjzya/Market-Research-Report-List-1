<p><strong>The Wind Turbine Drivetrain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Drivetrain</li><li>Direct Drivetrain</li><li>Multiple Generator Drivetrain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wind turbine drivetrain market consists of three main types - conventional drivetrain, direct drivetrain, and multiple generator drivetrain. Conventional drivetrain includes a gearbox to convert low-speed rotation into high-speed rotation for the generator. Direct drivetrain eliminates the gearbox, connecting the rotor directly to the generator. Multiple generator drivetrain uses multiple generators to capture energy from different areas of the rotor, increasing efficiency. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks, catering to different needs in the wind energy industry.</p></p>
