<p><strong>The Compressed Air Reel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-retracting</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed air reels are available in two main types: self-retracting and manual. Self-retracting reels automatically retract the hose back into the reel after use, providing convenience and preventing tangles or damage. Manual reels require the user to manually wind the hose back onto the reel after each use. Self-retracting reels are popular in industrial settings where frequent use of compressed air is required, while manual reels are commonly used in smaller-scale applications. Both types offer efficient storage and protection for compressed air hoses.</p></p>
