<p><ul><li>Half-finger Bicycle Gloves</li><li>Full-finger Bicycle Gloves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bicycle gloves come in two main types: half-finger and full-finger. Half-finger gloves provide extra grip and protection for the palms while leaving the fingers exposed for better flexibility and ventilation. They are preferred by cyclists looking for a balance between protection and breathability during warm weather rides. On the other hand, full-finger gloves provide complete coverage for the hands, offering increased protection against cuts, scrapes, and cold weather. They are favored by cyclists seeking maximum hand protection and warmth in various riding conditions.</p></p>
