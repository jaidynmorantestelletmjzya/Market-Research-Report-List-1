<p><p>Alzheimer's Disease Drugs market includes two main types of medications: Cholinesterase Inhibitors and NMDA Antagonists. Cholinesterase Inhibitors work by increasing levels of neurotransmitters in the brain that are important for memory and learning. NMDA Antagonists block the action of a neurotransmitter called glutamate, which is thought to contribute to brain cell damage in Alzheimer's disease. Both types of drugs aim to slow down the progression of symptoms and improve cognitive function in patients with Alzheimer's disease.</p></p>
