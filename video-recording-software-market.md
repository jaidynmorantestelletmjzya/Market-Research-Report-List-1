<p><p>Video recording software market types can be classified into two categories: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based video recording software operates on remote servers, enabling users to store and access videos online. It offers scalability, mobility, and ease of integration with other cloud-based applications. On the other hand, on-premises video recording software requires local installation on the user's hardware and operates within their network. It offers greater control, security, and customization options, but lacks the flexibility and accessibility of cloud-based solutions. These market types cater to different user preferences and requirements.</p></p>
