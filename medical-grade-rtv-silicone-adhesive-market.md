<p><p>The medical grade RTV silicone adhesive market consists of two main types: single component silicone adhesive and double component silicone adhesive. Single component silicone adhesives are pre-cured and require no mixing, offering easy application and faster curing times. On the other hand, double component silicone adhesives require a mixing process before application, providing stronger bonding capabilities and greater flexibility in terms of curing time. Both types of adhesives are commonly used in medical applications due to their biocompatibility and ability to withstand sterilization.</p></p>
