<p><strong>The High Tack Pressure Sensitive Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Hot-Melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High tack pressure sensitive adhesives have various types based on their composition: solvent-based, water-based, and hot-melts. Solvent-based adhesives use chemicals to bring the adhesive to its tacky state. Water-based adhesives use water as a carrier for the adhesive, making them environmentally friendly. Hot-melts are solid at room temperature but melt when heated, providing instant tack upon application. Each type of adhesive has its own advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
