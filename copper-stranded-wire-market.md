<p><strong>The Copper Stranded Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Copper Stranded Wire</li><li>Soft Copper Stranded Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid Copper Stranded Wire refers to wires that are made of a single copper core without any gaps or spaces in between the strands. This type of wire is known for its high mechanical strength and durability. </p><p>Soft Copper Stranded Wire, on the other hand, consists of multiple smaller copper strands twisted or braided together. This type of wire is more flexible and easier to bend or manipulate compared to solid copper wire. It is commonly used in applications where flexibility is required.</p></p>
