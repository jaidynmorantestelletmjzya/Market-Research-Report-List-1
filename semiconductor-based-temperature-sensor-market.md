<p><p>The Semiconductor-based Temperature Sensor market includes various types such as Voltage Output, Current Output, Digital Output, Resistance Output Silicon, and Diode Temperature Sensors. Voltage Output sensors provide a linear output voltage proportional to temperature, while Current Output sensors offer a linear output current. Digital Output sensors provide temperature readings in a digital format, while Resistance Output Silicon sensors change resistance with temperature. Diode Temperature Sensors utilize the temperature-dependent voltage drop across a diode to measure temperature accurately. These sensors cater to a wide range of industries and applications.</p></p>
