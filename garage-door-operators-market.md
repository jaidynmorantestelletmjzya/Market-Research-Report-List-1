<p><p>The Garage Door Operators Market includes three main types: Chain Driven, Screw Driven, and Belt Driven Garage Door Openers. Chain Driven openers operate using a chain mechanism for lifting the door. Screw Driven openers use a threaded steel rod to lift the door. Belt Driven openers use a rubber belt for operation, providing quieter and smoother operation compared to chain drive. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different preferences and requirements of consumers in the garage door operator market.</p></p>
