<p><p>Silicone Coated Release Film is available in different types based on the substrate material used. These types include PET Substrate, PE Substrate, PP Substrate, and Others. PET Substrate release films are made from polyester material, offering high tensile strength and durability. PE Substrate release films are made from polyethylene, known for its flexibility and low moisture absorption. PP Substrate release films are made from polypropylene, providing good chemical resistance and heat stability. Other substrates may include materials like nylon or aluminum foil.</p></p>
