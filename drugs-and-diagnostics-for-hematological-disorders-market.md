<p><p>The Drugs and Diagnostics for Hematological Disorders market includes two major types: Analyzers and Reagents. Analyzers are electronic devices used to measure and analyze various parameters of blood samples, such as white blood cell count, red blood cell count, and platelet count. They provide accurate and quick results, enabling proper diagnosis of hematological disorders. On the other hand, Reagents are substances used in combination with analyzers to detect and identify specific components or abnormalities in the blood. They play a crucial role in making accurate diagnoses and monitoring the progress of hematological disorders.</p></p>
