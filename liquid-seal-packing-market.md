<p><p>Liquid seal packing materials come in various types based on their composition. Resin type packing is made of synthetic materials that offer good resistance to chemicals and high temperatures. Rubber type packing is made of elastomers that provide flexibility and resilience. Oil modified type packing is made by combining oils with solid materials for improved durability. Natural polymer type packing is made from natural substances such as cellulose or starch, offering eco-friendly properties. Each type serves different industrial and commercial applications based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
