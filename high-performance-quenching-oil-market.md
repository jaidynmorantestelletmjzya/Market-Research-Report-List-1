<p><p>High Performance Quenching Oil market offers two main types - Straight Mineral Oil Type and Compounded Type. Straight Mineral Oil Type is formulated using only mineral oils, providing efficient cooling with uniform hardening. Compounded Type, on the other hand, combines mineral oils with additives like polymer quenchants or surface-active agents to enhance cooling rates and minimize distortion. Both types cater to different industrial needs, with Straight Mineral Oil Type offering simplicity and reliability, while Compounded Type provides advanced cooling capabilities.</p></p>
