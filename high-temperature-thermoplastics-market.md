<p><p>High Temperature Thermoplastics are polymers that can withstand high temperatures without losing their mechanical properties. High Temperature Fluoropolymers (High Temperature FPs) have excellent chemical resistance and high thermal stability. High Performance Polyamide (HPPA) offers great strength and toughness at high temperatures. Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) has good chemical resistance and high thermal stability. Sulfone Polymers (SP) have high impact resistance and excellent hydrolytic stability. Liquid Crystal Polymers (LCP) offer high heat resistance and dimensional stability. Aromatic Ketone Polymers (AKP) provide superior mechanical properties at high temperatures. Poly-imide (PI) has excellent thermal and chemical resistance properties.</p></p>
