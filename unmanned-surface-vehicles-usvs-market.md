<p><p>Wave-powered and propeller-driven unmanned surface vehicles (USVs) are two main types in the market. Wave-powered USVs utilize the energy generated by ocean waves to propel themselves, providing a sustainable and environmentally friendly option. On the other hand, propeller-driven USVs use traditional propellers to move through the water efficiently. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, with wave-powered USVs being favored for their renewable energy source and propeller-driven USVs known for their reliability and speed.</p></p>
