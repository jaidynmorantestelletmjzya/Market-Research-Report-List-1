<p><strong>The Polyester Artificial Marble Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pour Forming</li><li>Compression Molding</li><li>Bulk Scrap Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyester Artificial Marble market consists of three types: Pour Forming, Compression Molding, and Bulk Scrap Molding. Pour Forming involves pouring a mixture of polyester resin, marble powder, and color pigments into molds to create marble slabs. Compression Molding uses pressure to compress the mixture into desired shapes. Bulk Scrap Molding involves recycling scrap materials from previous production to create new marble products. Each method offers unique benefits and characteristics for producing high-quality artificial marble products.</p></p>
