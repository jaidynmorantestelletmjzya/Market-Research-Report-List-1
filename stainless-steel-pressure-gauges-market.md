<p><p>Stainless Steel Pressure Gauges are available in different market types, including Lower Mount, Back Mount, and Panel Mount. Lower Mount gauges are designed with the connection at the bottom, enabling a direct mounting position. Back Mount gauges have the connection at the back, suitable for installations where the pressure needs to be measured from the rear side. Panel Mount gauges are designed for installation on a panel or control panel, allowing easy monitoring in industrial settings. These market types offer versatility in gauge positioning and are used in various applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
