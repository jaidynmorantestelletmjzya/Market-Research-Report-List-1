<p><p>Inorganic Metal Finishing Market Types refer to various processes used to modify the surface of metal products. Anodizing involves creating a protective oxide layer on the metal surface using an electrolytic process. Cladding refers to coating a metal substrate with a different metal for enhanced protection or aesthetics. Conversion coatings are chemical treatments that result in a protective layer on the metal surface. Electroplating involves depositing a thin layer of metal onto another metal surface using an electrical current. Electroless plating is a similar process but does not require an electrical current. Other market types include processes like passivation and powder coating.</p></p>
