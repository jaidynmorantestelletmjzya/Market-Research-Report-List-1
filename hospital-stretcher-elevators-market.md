<p><p>Hospital stretcher elevators come in two main types: Automatic elevators and Semi-automatic elevators. These elevators are equipped with advanced technology that allows them to move the stretcher smoothly and efficiently without the need for manual control. In contrast, semi-automatic elevators require some level of manual operation, such as pushing buttons to control the movement of the stretcher. Both types of elevators serve the same purpose of transporting patients safely and conveniently within a hospital setting.</p></p>
