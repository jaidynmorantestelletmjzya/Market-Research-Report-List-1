<p><p>An Integrated Video Surveillance System is a security solution that combines various components for effective monitoring and recording of video footage. The Network Video Recorder (NVR) System uses IP cameras to record and store footage digitally, allowing for easy access and viewing over a network. On the other hand, the Digital Video Recorder (DVR) System uses analog cameras to capture footage, which is then converted to a digital format for storage and playback. Both systems are widely used in commercial and residential settings for security purposes.</p></p>
