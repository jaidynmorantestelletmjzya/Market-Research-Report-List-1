<p><p>The advanced balloon catheter market includes different types of catheters that are used for various medical procedures. Normal balloon catheters are used to dilate narrow or blocked blood vessels. Cutting and scoring balloon catheters have additional features like blades or scoring elements to treat calcified or fibrotic lesions. Drug-coated balloon catheters are designed to deliver medication to the target site to prevent restenosis. Stent graft balloon catheters are used for the placement of stent grafts in aorta or other blood vessels to treat aneurysms or dissections.</p></p>
